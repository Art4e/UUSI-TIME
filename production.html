NATURAL EUROPEAN FURS

FAMILY PRODUCTION

Our family-owned factory is located in Vaikna-Allika Talu, in an amazing place, famous for the cleanliness of the surrounding nature and the cold climate, which is so important in the production of high-quality and exquisite fur products.

Each product created by our craftsmen is custom-made to the highest standards.

All products are unique. For each bag, we individually select accessories, fabric trim tabs and accessories.

By purchasing our bag, you get unique with it and join the world of fresh natural materials and high quality of life.

DRESSING AND PAINTING

Fur products are not just beautiful, they shimmer in the light, play countless shades of color, pleasing to the eye.

But in order to achieve this luxurious effect, the dressing of hides and furs must be done correctly - violation of the technology will lead to coarsening of the skin, the fibers may become fragile, excessively soft and the product will begin to tear.

The workshop for the dressing and dyeing of natural fur UAB “Kailenos kailiai” (www.kailenoskailiai.lt) professionally processes the skins for future use.

The knowledge and technology of specialists and masters made it possible to achieve high results in this work.

When processing fur, all technological processes are maintained, which give the final result as a fur product that meets the highest quality standards.

The whole production is equipped with modern equipment, which allows you to make the dressing of the skin more thorough and less time-consuming.

The result exceeds all expectations of even the most demanding customers!
