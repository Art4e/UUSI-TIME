NOTICE

The shipment options are based on your delivery address and you will see delivery options and fees at the checkout before accepting order and making payment.

There might be limitations on parcel weight and size for each and any of delivery methods. If the weight and/or size exceed the transportation limits, we can reject the shipment and return the payment, or we can ask to pay extra charge if you accept. We will contact you in any case if there is such a necessity.

When the shipment is processed, the tracking number is provided in order to follow your shipment.

The shipment period is dependent on availability of items in stock. If the item is in stock in Riga, it will be shipped the same day after processing of payment. The shipment may delay 2-5 days if the item is not available in Riga stock.

OMNIVA and DPD Parcel Terminals (only Estonia & Lithuania)

There are number of OMNIVA and DPD Parcel Terminals available in Estonia and Lithuania.

The approximate delivery period is 2-5 days (if item is in Riga warehouse), 3 - 8 days (if item is main warehouse).

Express Mail (onLy in Estonia and Lithuania)

Very fast and comfortable door to door delivery service. Express Mail can ship parcels up to 5 kg.

The approximate delivery period is 2-5 days (if item is in Riga warehouse), 3 - 8 days (if item is main warehouse).

International Mail (Worldwide Shipping)

International Mail can ship parcels up to 2 kg. If you are interested to buy goods for more than 2 kg, please write us inquiry e-mail to info@darel.lv and describe your requirements.

The approximate delivery period is 5-15 days to Europe Union, 10-30 days outside Europe Union.

PAYMENT OPTIONS

You can choose any of the following payment options:

  1. Bank transfer (bank wire payment). You will receive all payment details by e-mail just after you proceed the order. Your order will be processed after reception of payment into our bank account.
  2. Online Bank payment via Paysera payment system provides following options:
    • Online bank payment (direct payment) for SEB, SWEDBANK, CITADELE and LUMINOR banks
    • Payment with your Credit or Debit card
  3. PayPal payment services:
    • PayPal payment via your PayPal account
    • Payment with your credit or debit card.
